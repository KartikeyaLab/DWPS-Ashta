Rajiv Soni- President of the Dipsite Association; Dr, Rakshanda Jalil, Historian, Sharmila Tagore, Actor and several others.

Our Thoughts

Why Choose Us?

The core objective of our school is to enlarge a child’s world and introduce it to the world beyond academics, we do not compromise over any pillar of quality education for there better future.

Our Mission

DWPS, Ashta is Committed to ‘Create a community of life-long learner’ who are inclined to learn the Right. Therefore , our constant efforts are to ignite the flame of inquisitiveness among our children.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to provide state of the art inclusive education where Indian Values and World Culture go hand in hand to create global citizens as torch bearers of a dynamic and harmonious world.

Academic

Our Academics

Our Educational Philosophy revolves around 3 E’s i.e. Experiment, Exploration and Experience which eventually lead to excellence.

Wonderland

‘Wonderland’ is an artistically painted, comfortable space for the kids, providing impetus in their preparatory years before formal education. The curriculum includes a proper balance between what is being taught so that every child understands what he or she is studying.

Primary Wing

The Primary years of formal Schooling are very crucial and play a vital role in establishing the personality of any child. Thus to ensure the board – based development of pupils and to enhance their emotional, cultural and physical skills and to prepare them for their future.

Know More

Middle Wing

Middle Wing education is the crux of Schooling. Where students develop self-reliance, can set their goals & interest. Therefore we DWPS- Ashta, delicately ensure that every child feels comfortable to added load of Subjects & curriculum enhancement at the Middle Level.

Know More

Senior Wing

The Senior School is home to students in Grades 9-11 and is characterized by advanced inquiry, lively discussion, deep engagement with topics, and a strong sense of community. The Senior School’s class periods provide time for broad exploration of ideas within each class.

Clubs

Our Clubs

This group spearheads a mission to achieve excellence through cutting edge learning tools combined with the human touch and the unique Guru – Shishya ( Teacher – Pupil ) bond, to integrate the best ideas in education taken from all good practices in India and around the World.

Dr. Kailash Satyarthi Wing

The Social Service Club is established to inculcate social behavior among our students. The activities of the Club teach students to be concerned not only about themselves but also for the welfare of the society as a whole. This inculcates community spirit in the students.

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Wing

This Club inculcates interest in the subject area taught in the school. The main function of this club is to create explorers who are always bubbling with the questions like ‘why’ and ‘how’ and to explore there imagination with talented mind, Opening there mind to change the world

Dr. R.N. Tagore Wing

Literary Club focuses on ensuring that children are ready to take their examinations with a rich vocabulary, genuine comprehension skills. The Club activity also develop public speaking skill among the children and an interest in reading for pleasure.

Dr. Ravi Verma Wing

Creativity Club proposes to discover and hone the latent talents of the students. The club provides adequate opportunities to bring their talents to the fore and thereby gain conﬁdence and recognition.

Dr. Saalim Ali Wing

Nature and Environment Club is being set up to train students how to safeguard the wild life and ecosystem in the face of climatic changes. This has become a priority for the sake of conservation and resource management.

Dhayan Chand Wing

A healthy mind resides in a healthy body. Sports Club, through its various sports activities delelops team spirit among Students and simultaneously improves their physical ﬁtness.

Sitara Devi Wing

Music is a healer which affects the heart, soul and mind. The Club, through performing art, enhances the potential of students to explore their latent talent.
