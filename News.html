Republic Day Celebration

"Freedom in Mind, Faith in Words, Pride in our Heart, Memories in our Souls."

The 74th Republic Day of India was celebrated at Delhi World Public School Ashta on 26 January 2023 with a great deal of patriotic zeal and fervor. To mark the day a huge bus rally was commenced and was lead throughout Ashta.

School Directors-Syed Parvez Ali,Mrs.Payal Ali,Mr.Bahadur Singh Sendhav and other honorable dignatories were welcomed by the school band the celebration was followed by hoisting our Indian tricolor flag .Further, to mark the importance of this day, a cultural programme was organized in the school premises by the students which included Saraswati Vandana, English and Hindi speeches,Mashup orchestra on Patriotic songs,Taekwondo fight Performances, Patriotic dance and other scintillating performances by our scholars that concluded with the thank you speech by School Head Girl with sweets distribution.The students and the staff took pride in glorifying and celebrating the spirit of unity by their performances. The audience were enthralled by the mesmerizing performances.
