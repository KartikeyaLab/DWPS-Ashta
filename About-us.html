Our Establishment

Establishment

Year of Establishment of School – 2017 , Run-2018

Emphasis is laid on learning from the environment combining spiritual philosophies with different subjects in the curriculum. To achieve this blend, our student community is involved in peer tutoring to reach out to fellow students who may be less privileged than them: our teachers are provided opportunities for researching pedagogic systems, and for continuous training and learning.

In addition to excellent teaching and learning facilities each of the school of Delhi World Foundation is encouraged to offer a variety of creative fields. And just as every Delhi World Public School is special, every student of our school is nurtured and empowered to blossom in his or her own special way.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vission
The goal of our organization is to provide comprehensive education that is geared toward creating global citizens through the exchange of Indian values and world cultures. We at Delhi World Public School, Ashta are dedicated to providing our students with the best possible education. We aim to make them successful and good people.
Our Mission
We Commite to ‘Create a community of life-long learner’ who are inclined to learn the Right. Therefore ,our constant efforts are to ignite the flame of inquisitiveness among our students so that when they leave the School, they should have developed the balance between head and heart and serve the Humanity as well.
