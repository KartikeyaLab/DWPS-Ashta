Our
Faculties

Together Everyone Achieves More' is the secret of DWPS ASHTA. A team of committed, diligent and devoted teachers is the strength of DWPS. It is due to the sheer consistency & perseverance that DWPS is able to lay the foundation of excellence and meet the growing challenges of time. The school boasts of qualified and competent teachers with years of teaching experience in India and abroad. The faculty members encourage updating their skills and knowledge on a regular basis. Besides, the teachers undergo regular training sessions in areas such as teaching methodologies and use of technology in the class.
